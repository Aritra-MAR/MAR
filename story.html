The crazy Monkey experiment

A scientist put five monkeys in a cage and placed, a ladder with bananas on the top in the middle of cage.
Every time a monkey would try to went up to the ladder, the scientist would soak the rest of the monkeys with ice cold water.
So the next time a monkey went up the ladder, the others beat up the one on the ladder thus taking the monkey down.
After a while, no monkey dared to go up the ladder regardless of the temptation.
The scientist then replaced one of the monkeys.This new monkey also tried to climb the ladder only to get beaten by the other monkeys.
After few beatings, the new monkey realized not to climb the ladder even though he never knew the reason.
A second monkey was substituted and the same scenairio occurred.
The first monkey also participated on the beating of the second monkey. A third monkey was changed and the same process was repeated (beating).
The fourth was replaced and the beating was repeated and finally the fifth monkey was replaced.
What left was a group of five monkeys that even though never received a ice cold shower(the reason not to climb ladder),
continued to beat up any monkey who attempted to climb the ladder.
If it was possible to ask the monkeys why they would beat up all those who attempted to
climb the ladder ... Perhaps the answer would be ... "I don't know —maybe that's how things are done here"
Whether it career or choices we make, we subconsciously just walk the track laid down by others(society) without even questioning.
If only we could dare to question and lay our own path.
