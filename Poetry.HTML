 The Price We pay

I wonder, how do they spend life

those who pay for the predilection.

When no one stops off to visit

Roof and door must make noise,


Do the paths ever end?


Surely they go somewhere else passing by my house.

There is a continuous knock
My tests must be calling me.

 

Now the citizens of this city

must be settling in another city,

probably somewhere I am also seen.

There is a continuous knock
My tests must be calling me.
